# Rules and Regulations

This section contains specific rules and regulations established by the Franklin Square Homeowners Association Board of Directors to govern daily operations and community standards.

These rules complement the Declaration, Bylaws, and Articles of Incorporation, and are adopted under the authority granted to the Board in the governing documents.

## Available Rules

The following rules and standards are currently in effect:

- **[Pool Rules](pool-rules.html)** - Rules governing the use of community pool facilities, including hours, guest policies, safety requirements, and prohibited activities

- **[Architectural Control Standards](architectural-control-standards.html)** - Guidelines and approval requirements for exterior modifications to homes and property, including landscaping, painting, additions, and other improvements

## About These Rules

Rules and regulations may be updated by the Board of Directors as needed to address changing community needs and circumstances. Homeowners should familiarize themselves with all applicable rules.

Violations of community rules may result in warnings, fines, or other enforcement actions as outlined in the governing documents.

## Questions About Rules

If you have questions about specific rules or need clarification on whether a planned project requires architectural approval, please contact the HOA Board of Directors.
