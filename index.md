# Franklin Square Homeowners Association

Welcome to the digital archive of legal documents for the Franklin Square Homeowners Association, Inc., located in Palm Harbor, Florida.

## About This Site

This is an unofficial resource that provides Franklin Square homeowners with accessible, searchable copies of the community's governing legal documents. All documents are maintained in a version-controlled format, allowing residents to:

- Easily reference HOA rules, regulations, and bylaws
- Understand their rights and responsibilities as homeowners
- Review the historical evolution of community governance through amendments
- Search across all documents for specific topics or terms

## Quick Access to Key Documents

### Governing Documents

- **[Declaration](declaration.html)** - The Declaration of Covenants, Conditions, and Restrictions (CC&Rs) governing the community
- **[Bylaws](bylaws.html)** - Operational rules and procedures for the HOA Board of Directors and membership
- **[Articles of Incorporation](articles-of-incorporation.html)** - The founding document establishing the Association as a legal entity

### Additional Documentation

- **[Amendments](amendments/)** - Chronological amendments to governing documents (1986-2022)
- **[Rules and Regulations](rules-and-regulations/)** - Specific community rules including pool rules and architectural control standards

## Using This Site

### Search Functionality
Use the search bar at the top of the page to find specific topics, terms, or clauses across all documents. The search covers the full text of every document on this site.

### Navigation
Use the sidebar menu to browse documents by category. Documents are organized by type for easy reference.

## Important Legal Disclaimer

{: .warning }
> **This is an unofficial, volunteer-maintained resource. These documents are provided for reference purposes only.**
> 
> While every effort has been made to accurately transcribe these legal documents, they should not be relied upon for legal purposes without verification. The language in all documents is preserved exactly as it appears in the original legal documents, including original capitalization, punctuation, and any apparent inconsistencies.
> 
> For legal purposes, please:
> - Consult the official recorded copies with Pinellas County or HOA management
> - Verify the language against authoritative sources
> - Seek appropriate legal counsel when needed

## Questions or Corrections?

If you notice any discrepancies between these documents and the official recorded versions, please contact the HOA Board of Directors or email cdlgeneral@protonmail.com.
