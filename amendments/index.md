# Amendments to Governing Documents

This section contains all amendments to the Franklin Square Homeowners Association's Declaration of Covenants, Conditions, and Restrictions (CC&Rs) and other governing documents.

Amendments are listed in chronological order, from earliest to most recent. Each amendment modifies specific sections of the original governing documents and has been properly recorded with Pinellas County, Florida.

## Available Amendments

The following amendments have been adopted by the Association:

1. **[June 2, 1986](1986-06-02.html)** - First recorded amendment
2. **[April 10, 1987](1987-04-10.html)** - Second amendment
3. **[January 24, 1994](1994-01-24.html)** - Third amendment
4. **[August 24, 1998](1998-08-24.html)** - Fourth amendment
5. **[October 7, 2003](2003-10-07.html)** - Fifth amendment
6. **[April 7, 2011](2011-04-07.html)** - Sixth amendment
7. **[October 10, 2018](2018-10-10.html)** - Seventh amendment
8. **[January 31, 2022](2022-01-31.html)** - Most recent amendment (Parking restrictions)

## Understanding Amendments

Each amendment document includes:
- The specific article and section being amended
- The complete new text replacing the original language
- Recording information for Pinellas County records

When reviewing the governing documents, always consider amendments that may have modified the original language. The most recent amendment takes precedence over earlier versions.
